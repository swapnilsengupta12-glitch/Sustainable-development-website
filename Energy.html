Energy Conservation

Our energy consumption has a direct link to greenhouse gas emissions. Conserving energy is one of the easiest ways to fight climate change.

At Home:

  • Turn off lights when you leave a room.
  • Unplug electronics when they are not in use (vampire power).
  • Use energy-efficient appliances and LED light bulbs.

In Transport:

  • Walk or cycle for short distances.
  • Use public transportation whenever possible.
  • Consider carpooling with friends or family.
