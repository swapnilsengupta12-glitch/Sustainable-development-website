Water Conservation

Freshwater is a finite and precious resource. Every drop saved helps maintain healthy ecosystems and ensures a steady supply for everyone.

  • Take shorter showers instead of long baths.
  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth or washing dishes.
  • Fix any leaky faucets.
  • Water plants in the morning or evening to minimize evaporation.
